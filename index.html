How EarthlyData Works

What is Earthly Data?

Earthly Data is a satellite observation platform that collects and analyzes satellite imagery and environmental data to monitor blooming events, weather conditions, and pest occurrences. We transform NASA datasets into clear, actionable information for researchers, farmers, and anyone interested in understanding environmental change.

Relation to SDG 15

This project supports Sustainable Development Goal 15 — Life on Land — by providing data-driven insights that help protect biodiversity and promote the sustainable management of terrestrial ecosystems.

Impact

The impact of Earthly Data lies in strengthening scientific research, supporting sustainable agricultural decision-making, and raising awareness about the effects of climate change on terrestrial ecosystems. By improving timing for planting and pest management and informing conservation planning, the platform fosters resilience in both agriculture and natural habitats.

Why it matters

Ecological research

Helps scientists understand the effects of climate on ecosystems and biodiversity through floral phenology.

Agricultural management

Enables farmers to optimize planting, harvesting, and pest control according to natural blooming cycles.

Conservation and planning

Provides information to make informed decisions on species conservation and sustainable land management.
